About

I'm Kylie! My connection to the islands takes roots in my identity as a hula dancer of 14+ years and my many trips back to O`ahu where my family resides.

CalpoLEI is an idea that formed as a result of my love for Hawaii and Hawaiian culture + a few haku lei I made for my friends' graduation and birthdays. With their support and encouragment, I started to see and construct a concept of haku lei making as an exciting venture.

Perpetuating authentic Hawaiian tradition is fundemental to preserving the culture. I find so much joy in sharing my appreciation of Hawaii through hula dancing, and now by teaching and gifting others the craft of haku lei.
